    Terminology

    Source

    A detection in a certain wavelength significant enough to be recorded in a catalogue with sky position.

    Counterpart

    The corresponding source in another catalogue.

    Association

    A specific combination of entries from the various catalogues, i.e. a tuple of detections associated with each other.

    Match

    Same as association, used interchangeably.

    Object

    A physical entity in the real universe, emitting radiation.
